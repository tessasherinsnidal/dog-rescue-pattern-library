About Us

We are a highly experienced, educated and knowledgeable Great Dane and Giant Canine Breed Rescue plus also assisting Equines, BHRR has a strong focus on the Special Needs – (Deaf and/or Blind, Sick, Senior & Injured)

*Reputable/Quality Rescue is about helping the next one in need*

Giant Breeds BHRR Assists: Deerhound, Dogue de Bordeaux, English Mastiff, Great Dane, Neapolitan Mastiff, Newfoundland, & Irish Wolfhound

Our area of rescue assistance is across North America & our area of adoption placement is ONLY in Ontario, Quebec and some parts of NY, USA

We also provide a safe ‘haven’ for animals for life should they not be adopted or are deemed un-adoptable

BHRR is a federally registered non-profit group and we are an all Volunteer, Foster Home based rescue without any salaried employees
